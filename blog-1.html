Week 1

Below are our initial concept sketches. We decided to explore different ways to help users with limited hand dexterity, such as those with arthritis. In the end we decided to move forward with the cutting board concept.

CONCEPT ONE

For our first concept, we designed an assistive card holder to allow the user to hold cards securely without needing to grip them between their thumb and fingers.

CCONCEPT TWO

In the second concept, we thought of an assistive spoon holder that allows the users to perform natural eating or drinking motions without having to grip the utensil in the user's hand.

CONCEPT THREE

Our third concpet is a hands-free light that wraps around the user's neck so that they can walk without having to hold onto the light source.

CONCEPT FOUR

Four our fourth concept, we designed a scoop that allows the user to slide the item in and press down to pick up it up.

CONCEPT FIVE

After iterating on concept four, we though that it was more useful to make it into a cutting board where the users could hold the food in place while cutting. We decided to continue with this concept.
