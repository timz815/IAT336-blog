Week 3

After feedback during lab, we redesigned the holder to be modular and cleanable so it could be positioned in multiple places on the board and different sizes. We purposefully designed the shape of the holder so that once it is placed down in position, it would be hard move around unless picking it back up, which makes it perfect when the user needs to hold something.
