Assistive Cutting Board

An innovative approach to making food preparation more accessible for individuals with limited mobility or dexterity.

Project Updates

Week 2

After deciding to move forward with the assistive cutting board concept, we transitioned from ideation to hands on exploration through low fidelity prototyping. We used styrene, foa, cardstock, and cardboard to build multiple phsyical models to better understand structural integriy, ergonomics, and dimensional relationships
