Week 4

With more feedback during lab, we redesigned the board so that it could be picked up with out the holders falling out by narrowing the bottom, while still leaving a gap so it remained cleanable.

From last week’s testing, we also found that the original holders weren’t very effective at securing food, so we developed a new arch concept that was much better at holding the food down. As well since the holders could not move, we created a smaller cutting board insert that could slide in and out for repositioning the food. We also experimented with felt to test if the making it gripper to the table would improve usability.
